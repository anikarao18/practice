Eloheh Management Team

At Eloheh, we strive to deliver products to you with compassion, innovation, and trust. Our management team brings together years of experience in healthcare, wellness, and first-hand experiences with patients.

These passionate individuals are the driving factor behind our mission to provide for all communities.




Meet the Team Behind Eloheh

Dr. Stacy Rodgers - Chief Medical Officer

"Healthcare is not simply about selling products to treat illnesses, it is about building trust, one person at a time"

Dr. Rodgers has over 30 years of medical expertise in community-based care and creating preventative medications. As the founder, she leads our team with prestige care and empathy which is the driving force to our success.

Dr. Micheal Smith - Operational Director

Dr. Smith ensures that all of our products are provided to our patients seamlessly. With a background in healthcare logistics, his focus guarantees that every step of the process at Eloheh runs smoothly.

Dr. Garrett Payne - Chief Technological Officer

Dr. Payne lead our digital transformation, ensuring that Eloheh is at the forefront of healthcare technology, always with the patients' safety in mind.
